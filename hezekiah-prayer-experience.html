The Hezekiah Prayer Experience

A deep journey through 2 Kings 19-20

1. The Threat (2 Kings 19:8-13)

Sennacherib sends a blasphemous letter. Hezekiah spreads it before the LORD. Spurgeon: “The best way to face the enemy is to put the letter in God’s hands.”

2. The Prayer (2 Kings 19:15-19)

“You alone are God.” Hebrew: בָּטַח (bāṭaḥ) – to trust completely. R.C. Sproul: “Prayer does not change God, it changes the one who prays.”

3. God’s Answer (2 Kings 19:32-36 & 20:5)

God sent an angel. “I have heard your prayer, I have seen your tears.” Reflection: Do you believe God sees your tears?

4. The Warning (2 Kings 20:12-19)

Hezekiah showed his treasures. David Guzik: “Blessing can become a test if we do not handle it with humility.”

“Lord, You alone are God. Amen.”
