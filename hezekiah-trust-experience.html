The Hezekiah Trust Experience

2 Kings 17-18 • A journey of faith in crisis

1. The Collapse of Israel (2 Kings 17)

In 722 B.C. Assyria destroyed Samaria. Why? “The children of Israel sinned against the LORD their God” (17:7). Historical fact: over 27,000 Israelites were deported. Reflection: What modern idols are destroying families today?

Hebrew word: מָעַל (ma‘al) – unfaithfulness, covenant betrayal.

2. Hezekiah’s Reform

Hezekiah removed the high places (18:4). Spurgeon: “Faith that does not remove idols is not true faith.”

3. Prayer in Crisis

Hezekiah prayed: “You alone are God” (19:15). R.C. Sproul: “Prayer does not change God, but it changes the one who prays.”

"Lord, like Hezekiah, I trust in You alone. Amen."
